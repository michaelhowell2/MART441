About Me

A Memorable Gaming Moment

My favorite gaming moment was the release of Star Wars Galaxies many many years ago. The game at the time was just amazing. Sandbox Star Wars games where one could live out their Star Wars fantasies to their own choosing. You could be a crafter, merchant, bounty hunter, almost anything your heart desired. Building and decorating houses, a unique crafting system, It was just awesome at the time! I met many lifelong friends through guilds. I still am in touch with a few of my gaming friends 20 or so years later..
